What we do

Five SMEs. Every downstream lever, from Brent diff to retail margin.

ContexAi's Energy & Petrochemicals Practice is built on five specialist Subject Matter Experts — Refining Economics, Petrochemical Value Chain, Asset Integrity & Reliability, Capital Projects, and Global Trading & Marine Logistics. Inspired by best-in-class operators (Saudi Aramco, Reliance Jamnagar, Valero, SABIC), each SME runs at the margin-lever level — LP / PIMS models, crack spreads, asset integrity by API 510 / 570 / 653, FEED through commissioning, and physical / paper trading.

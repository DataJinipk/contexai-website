What we do

Conventional, Islamic, and microfinance — all three banking grammars under one practice.

Pakistani banking is three industries in a trench coat — conventional commercial banks, Islamic banks (and conversion windows), and microfinance banks regulated under the SBP MFB Prudential Regulations. Generic advisory firms force one playbook on all three. ContexAi runs three sub-practices with senior partners in each, supported by 12+ Agentic AI skills covering PIBAS core banking, IFRS 9 ECL modeling, SBP regulatory submissions, and Shariah-product structuring.

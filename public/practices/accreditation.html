What we do

The full lifecycle of a Pakistani professional body — from registration to global recognition.

Standing up a new professional institute in Pakistan is a 4-workstream program — corporate (SECP), regulatory (SBP / PBA endorsements), academic (HEC accreditation), and IP (IPO-Pakistan trademark for the designation). ContexAi runs all four in parallel, with a master plan that sequences the critical path through SECP Section 42 NPO licensing — the bottleneck most consultants miss.

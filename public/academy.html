What is ContexAi Academy

Not another theory course. A studio for builders.

Every level pairs the concepts you need with a real lab and a project you keep. You learn by shipping — guided by an in-product AI tutor, an auto-grader, and a certificate that proves you finished.

The journey

Five levels, one outcome: you can build.

Level 1

Advanced prompting & local LLMs

Master chain-of-thought, tree-of-thought and directional-stimulus prompting, run open-source models locally with Ollama / llama.cpp, and tune temperature, top-p and penalties like a pro.

Prompt engineeringOllamaParameter tuning
Level 2

MCP integrations & voice agents

Integrate connectors with the Model Context Protocol — online and offline — then design system prompts that power fully autonomous, low-latency voice agents.

MCP serversstdio / SSEVoice agents
Level 3

Generative diffusion & AI clones

Understand latent diffusion, train LoRAs, build a consistent digital AI clone, and run pipelines for images, video, ads, branding material and cinematic cuts.

DiffusionLoRAVideo & ads
Level 4

Complex automations & agentic workflows

Build production-grade automations for process optimization and get hands-on with self-correcting AI agents using LangGraph, CrewAI and AutoGen.

Orchestrator–workersLangGraphCrewAI
Level 5

Ship a real AI product — no code

Scope, scaffold and launch a market-ready AI micro-SaaS using visual builders and automation wrappers — a 100% functional product, without a single line of backend code.

Bubble / FlutterFlowMake.com / n8nCapstone launch

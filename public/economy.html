What the budget means, in four lines

  • Consolidation with relief — outlay PKR 18.77trn, deficit 3.6% of GDP, 4th straight primary surplus, yet salaried & super-tax cuts.
  • Enforcement-led revenue — faceless audit, e-invoicing, production monitoring and a Central Data Hub carry a 17.6% FBR target.
  • Clear winners — construction & cement, IT, textiles/exporters, pharma, local auto assembly.
  • Relative losers — banks, E&P, fertilizer, branded FMCG/dairy, steel, debt funds and non-filers.

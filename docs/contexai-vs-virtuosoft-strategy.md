**Author:** Amir Waheed Ahmed, Founder & Creator, ContexAi Group
**Date:** 13 June 2026
**Status:** Draft for review
**Purpose:** Benchmark ContexAi's service offering against Virtuosoft (a regional reference) and lay out a phased plan to (a) embed new services that play to ContexAi's strengths and (b) revise the contexai.org information architecture to reflect them.

## 1. Virtuosoft — what they actually do

Virtuosoft positions itself as a **tech-first IT services firm** with Pakistan HQ and 5-country footprint (PK, KSA, UAE, France, USA). Leadership is anchored on **Muhammad Lukman** (Chairman, ex-CEO NCCPL for 20 years; ex-PwC) and **Shoaib ur Rehman** (CEO, tech delivery veteran). Their team is heavy on financial-sector tech (NCCPL alumni in IT Governance and Cybersecurity) and BPO/finance services (Sohail Malik, Head of Financial BPO).

### 1.1 Their site structure
- About Us / Services / Our Team / Careers / Contact
- Services is a flat mega-menu with **8 verticals** and **27 sub-services**
- Team page lists 10 people with role + bio + LinkedIn

### 1.2 Their 8 service verticals

| Vertical | Sub-services |
| --- | --- |
| **Advisory** | Product Strategy & Roadmapping · Digital Transformation |
| **Artificial Intelligence** | AI Agent · Generative AI · Data Science & MLOps · Conversational Intelligence · Computer Vision · OCR |
| **Software Engineering** | Custom Software Dev · App Dev (Mobile & Web) · App Modernization · Database Migration · Third-Party Integrations |
| **Cybersecurity** | Solution Deployment · Governance & Compliance · Cyber Assessments · Managed Cybersecurity Service · Risk Mgmt & Governance |
| **Data Service** | Data Strategy Consulting · Data Warehouse · Data Analytics & BI |
| **IT Governance** | Regulatory Compliance · Governance Framework Development · Resource Augmentation |
| **Solutions** | ERP · BMC Helix · MS Dynamics 365 · ManageEngine |
| **Expertise** | RPA · DevOps |

### 1.3 Read on their positioning
Virtuosoft is **delivery-heavy**, **vendor-aligned** (BMC, Microsoft, ManageEngine), and competes for systems-integration mandates. Their advisory line is shallow (only Product Strategy + Digital Transformation). Where they over-index: Cybersecurity (5 sub-services, anchored by an NCCPL ex-CISO) and AI (6 sub-services).

## 4. Recommended services to embed under ContexAi

The principle: **stay advisory-led and partner-neutral.** Add services that compound the existing senior-network advantage. Avoid vendor-aligned implementation work where ContexAi cannot win on scale.

### 4.1 Add — eight new service lines (priority order)

| # | New service line | Sub-services | Lead-time |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **1** | **Data & Decision Intelligence** | Data Strategy · Data Modeling & Warehouse Advisory · BI & Dashboarding (Power BI, Tableau, Sigma) · Decision Intelligence / Analytics Translation | Q3 2026 |
| **2** | **Cybersecurity & IT Risk Advisory** | IT Risk Assessment for FIs · SBP IT Risk Framework Compliance · Cybersecurity Governance for Banks/MFBs · ISO 27001 Readiness · Vendor Risk Reviews | Q3 2026 |
| **3** | **Technology Advisory & Modernization** | Core System Selection (PIBAS, T24, Finacle, Flexcube) · Application Modernization Strategy · Third-Party Integration Architecture · Vendor Selection & RFP Support | Q4 2026 |
| **4** | **Generative & Agentic AI Solutions** (rename from "Agentic AI") | Custom GPT/Claude Workflows · RAG Knowledge Agents · Document Intelligence (OCR + extraction) · Conversational Intelligence · Agent Orchestration | Q3 2026 |
| **5** | **Managed / Fractional Services** | Fractional CFO · Fractional CISO · Fractional Compliance Officer · Fractional Internal Audit · Managed Section-42 NPO Secretariat | Q4 2026 |
| **6** | **Resource Augmentation** ("SME-on-demand") | Embedded Subject Matter Experts · Embedded Agentic AI Builders · Project-based Specialist Pods · Interim Leadership | Q3 2026 (formalize what already happens) |
| **7** | **Regulatory & Governance Advisory** | SBP Banking Prudential Compliance · SECP Section 42 / NBFC Compliance · IPO-Pakistan Trademark Strategy · HEC Accreditation Support · Section 42 NPO Secretariat | Q3 2026 (CIP work already does this) |
| **8** | **Financial BPO** (light-touch) | Treasury Advisory · Tax Advisory Support · Bookkeeping QA · Year-end Close Support · Audit Support | Q1 2027 (optional) |

### 4.2 Do NOT add

- **ERP implementation** (Oracle, SAP, Dynamics) — we are not a Tier-1 SI
- **Vendor-aligned product implementations** (BMC Helix, ManageEngine) — locks us in
- **Custom mobile/web app development** as a primary line — referral instead
- **Generic RPA / DevOps** — commodity work that erodes the brand

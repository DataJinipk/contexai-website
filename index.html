About ContexAi Group

A dual mandate: advisory excellence & digital ventures

Founded by Amir Waheed Ahmed with 20+ years in financial consulting, ContexAi Group operates two integrated pillars. The advisory practice delivers strategic consulting, dispute resolution, and PPP advisory across Pakistan's most complex sectors. The ventures arm builds and incubates AI-powered digital businesses — from ride-hailing to agri-commerce — that form the ContexAi intelligent commerce ecosystem.
